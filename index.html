About

Engineering the future of Web3

Aman Vaths is the Founder & CTO of Nadcab Labs, a digital engineering firm specializing in AI, Web3, blockchain infrastructure, cloud, and cybersecurity. With roots in Allahabad and engineering education from MNNIT, he blends traditional values with cutting-edge technical insight.

His work sits at the intersection of identity, payments, decentralized finance, and sovereign blockchain architecture — emphasizing systems that work in production, not just demos. From stablecoin platforms and on-chain KYC networks to custom Layer-1/Layer-3 chains and high-concurrency blockchain architecture, Aman leads complex systems from design to deployment.
