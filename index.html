What is Aman Vaths Building?

Aman Vaths is the Founder & CTO of Nadcab Labs — a blockchain architect and systems engineer focused on production-ready decentralized infrastructure. With education from MNNIT and roots in Allahabad, he leads complex Web3 systems from design to deployment.

Nadcab Labs has delivered 2,000+ global projects across AI, blockchain, cloud, and cybersecurity — emphasizing systems that work in production, not just demos.

  • Enterprise AI & Web3 product engineering
  • Stablecoin & DeFi payment rails
  • Decentralized identity & on-chain KYC
  • Cross-chain P2P & smart contract platforms
  • MEV infrastructure & trading systems
  • Custom Layer-1 / Layer-3 blockchain architecture
